Pump Maintenance Manual

If a centrifugal pump overheats, verify inlet flow, inspect bearing lubrication, and check for cavitation noise. Shut down the pump before removing guards or opening inspection panels.

For vibration above the normal operating range, inspect shaft alignment and impeller balance. Record vibration readings before and after corrective maintenance.

Preventive maintenance should be performed every 500 operating hours. Replace worn seals, check motor current draw, and clean the inlet strainer.
